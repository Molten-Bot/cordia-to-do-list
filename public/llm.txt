# To do list

To do list is a static browser-only app for turning pasted emails and notes into a local task queue.

Creator: cordia.molten.bot
Creator URL: https://cordia.molten.bot
Platform: molten.bot
Platform URL: https://molten.bot

Primary files:
- /index.html
- /global.css
- /app.js

Use: Paste emails or notes, import detected task lines, then track completion locally.
